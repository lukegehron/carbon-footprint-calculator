Our story of reforestation in Haiti is about spirited CODEP people who have patiently built the soil, started seedlings, protected young plants, and built on the opportunities that their 15 million trees offer.

For three decades Haiti Reforestation Partnership has helped our Haitian partners plant 15 million trees across 5,578 acres of mountains. Help us continue to plant for the future.

Your donation is a gift to the people of Haiti, to all our children, and to the world.
